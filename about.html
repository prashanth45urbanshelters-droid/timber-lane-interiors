About Timberlane

Our Story

Interior design is more than aesthetics.
It's creating spaces that feel like you.

We create spaces that inspire, function beautifully, and reflect the people who live or work in them. Every project is a deeply personal collaboration between our designers and you.

Founded in 2018 in Bangalore, Timberlane Interiors has grown from a small studio into a team of 14 expert designers. We've completed over 200 projects, each one a testament to our belief that great design changes lives.
